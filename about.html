About Our Company

Professional services company

TransLang is an experienced U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) provider of Security Cooperation, Program Management, Training, Education, and Management Support Services. Our highly relevant experience, clearly demonstrating our exceptional qualifications to provide support to our government clients.

TransLang, Ltd. was established in Virginia in 2000 as a privately held, Woman-Owned, Small Business professional services company. We have supported Professional Military Institutions, Civilian Educational Centers and academic institutions for over 20 years including the two Regional Centers that are the subject of this RFI. Our SMEs and support personnel are experienced and well-known within the NESA and WJPC communities and have helped to develop much of the curriculum taught at these institutions. We believe this past experience and inherent knowledge enable us to provide the most qualified support services and personnel.

TransLang has been operating for over 22 years.
